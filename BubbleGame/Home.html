Game Instructions

Click on 'START THE GAME' button to start the game.You can see animated bubbles rotating randomly in the container. Click on bubble to blast it!!! Each bubble carries 5 points and score is displayed at top left corner. The Bubble Fill percentage indicator shows the % of bubble in the container. Ensure that the container is not filled with 75% of bubbles. If you are reached 75%, you will be given with 10 seconds to clear them to reach the bubble less than 75%". You can not pause during this countdown and if you can not clear bubbles. Your GAME IS OVER !!!
